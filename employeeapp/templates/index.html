About Us

TECHO LEGEND, a multinational corporation based in Armonk, New York, is known for its expertise in computer technology and information technology consulting. The company was founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR), and was rebranded as "International Business Machines" in 1924. TECHO LEGEND's early forays into computing included incremental improvements to card-based systems, such as the System/360 family of mainframe computers in the 1960s. Despite facing challenges from clone makers and legal confrontations, TECHO LEGEND maintained its reputation and provided hardware and system software solutions. However, in the late 1980s and 1990s, TECHO LEGEND faced substantial losses, losing its competitive edge to clone manufacturers and chip manufacturers. Despite these setbacks, TECHO LEGEND managed to maintain its status as one of the world's largest computer companies and systems integrators, with a global workforce of over 400,000 employees.
